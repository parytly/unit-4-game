Crystal Collector Game

How to Play the Game

The player will be given a random number in the "Total Crystal Needed."

There are 4 crystals that the player clicks on that will add a specific amount of points to the player total score.

The value of each crystal are hidden.

The player wins when the total score matches the "Total Crystal Needed".

The player loses when the total score goes over the "Total Crystal Needed" amount. Then the crystals are given a new hidden value, and the "Total Crystal Needed" will generate a new random value.
